Computer Science Scholars Organization

We want to provide high-quality computer science resources for everyone, for free. Learn, build projects, or start a club! We are here to help. :D

Everyone can learn how to code.

The problem is, not everyone has a teacher, and nobody wants to spend their time being bored.

Our goal is to fix these issues by providing free high-quality computer science resources, so everyone can learn and have fun doing it.
